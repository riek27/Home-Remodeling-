Our Story

We started our remodeling company with one mission: to transform homes with precision craftsmanship and thoughtful design. Founded in Houston over two decades ago, Aether Construction has grown from a small team of artisans into a premier luxury remodeling firm. Every project reflects our dedication to architectural elegance and client satisfaction.

Our Mission: To create timeless spaces where Houston families live, gather, and thrive — using premium materials and seamless project execution.

Our Commitment to Quality: From the initial sketch to the final walkthrough, we hold every detail to the highest standard. We believe a remodel should feel effortless and last a lifetime.
